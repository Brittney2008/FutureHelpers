Safety Tips

At Future Helpers, we strive to make youth volunteerism secure and enjoyable. Here are some crucial safety tips to guide you on your volunteer journey:

  • Know your rights: Be aware of your legal obligations when volunteering. Understand your duties and responsibilities, and don't hesitate to ask questions.
  • Stay Informed: Research organizations before committing to opportunities to ensure authenticity.
  • Work with Adults: Always volunteer in groups or under adult supervision to avoid unsafe situations.
  • Communicate: Keep your parents or guardians informed about your activities and seek their support.
