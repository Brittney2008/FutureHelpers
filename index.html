Welcome to Future Helpers

Welcome to Future Helpers, the website that gives under-18-year-old volunteers the tools they need to positively influence their communities! We have everything you need to make a virtual or local volunteer contribution from the comfort of your home. Our mission is to link youth with developmentally appropriate activities so they can learn new skills, give back, and change the world. We at Future Helpers think that the next generation will bring about change. Our well-curated list of volunteer opportunities and resources for safe and productive engagement make it simple for you to find local and global community service opportunities. One deed of kindness at a time, we can collectively mold a brighter future.
